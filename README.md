# Hi there, I'm Mic! 👋

I'm a **visual artist and director** running a video production business. My work lives at the intersection of AI, generative art, and workflow automation (**Vibe Coding**).

After years of experience in high-end video production — including creating custom scripts for After Effects — I now leverage LLMs to build browser-based, zero-backend tools. My mission is to bridge the gap between generative web art and professional broadcast pipelines.

### 🛠 What I'm up to:

* 🚀 **Currently building:** Custom web-to-video rendering engines and sophisticated UI layouts (like [Bento-Studio](https://github.com/BNWCREATIVE/Bento-Studio)).
* 🌱 **Exploring:** LLM-assisted development, canvas manipulation, and bypassing browser media constraints.
* 💬 **Ask me about:** Art direction, visual content, and creative automation.
